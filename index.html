HOW TO

In 3 simple steps

  • 1

    Add your files

    Drop files into RenameMe or click Add to browse. The app shows both original and new names side-by-side for clarity.

  • 2

    Build your workflow

    Stack operations like Replace, Trim, or Sequence. Each operation applies in order, and you see the results update live!

  • 3

    Click Rename to apply changes. Love your workflow? Save it for next time with a single click—no need to rebuild it!
